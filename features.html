Afraid you might end up with a nightmare roomie? No worries! Our rating system allows users to rate other users to you get vetted reviews of who you might end up with!
PERSONALITY MATCHING
When you sign up for Roomie, you are presented with a simple questionnaire. Then when you go searching, our system will intelligently match you to people that are similar to you. It couldn't be easier!
